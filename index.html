Amenities Rules Fishing Maps Location Info

Amenities

  • 1200 wooded acres
  • Over 700 acres of common ground
  • 10 private lakes for fishing and boating (10hp max)
  • Olympic-size swimming pool (Memorial Day–Labor Day)
  • Comfort stations with showers
  • Clubhouse and concession stand
  • Basketball, tennis, and pickleball courts
  • Sand volleyball court
  • ATV riding trails
  • Hiking and nature trails
  • Playgrounds and horseshoe pits
  • Outdoor chapel and memorial garden

Campground Rules

  • Quiet hours: Weekdays 10 PM – 8 AM; Weekends 11 PM – 9 AM
  • Fires only in fire rings
  • No unattended fires
  • Fully extinguish fires before leaving
  • No fireworks allowed
  • One camping unit per site
  • Keep vehicles within your site
  • Secure trash from wildlife
  • Return moved picnic tables

Fishing Information

Lake Access

  • 10 private lakes available for fishing
  • Missouri fishing regulations apply
  • Catch and release encouraged for bass
  • 10hp motor limit on all lakes
  • Please respect posted lake rules

Best Fishing Tips

  • Early morning and evening are most productive
  • Try soft plastics and spinnerbaits for bass
  • Use worms or minnows for panfish
  • Fish near fallen trees and shaded banks

Fishing Courtesy

  • Clean up all fishing line and trash
  • Respect other anglers' space
  • Follow campground quiet hours
