About Our Campground

Phesant 8 is a private recreational campground designed for families, RV owners, and outdoor lovers looking for a relaxing nature experience. The community features wooded acreage, private lakes, and year-round camping opportunities.

Amenities

  • 1200 wooded acres
  • Over 700 acres of common ground
  • 10 private lakes for fishing and boating (10hp motor max)
  • Olympic-size swimming pool (Memorial Day–Labor Day)
  • Comfort stations with showers
  • Clubhouse and concession stand
  • Baseball/softball field
  • Basketball, tennis, and pickleball courts
  • Sand volleyball court
  • ATV riding trails (400 acres)
  • Hiking and nature trails
  • Playgrounds and horseshoe pits
  • Outdoor chapel and memorial garden
  • Security and maintenance staff

Campground Rules

  • Quiet hours: Weekdays 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM; Weekends 11:00 PM – 9:00 AM
  • Fires allowed only in provided fire rings
  • No large bonfires
  • Do not leave fires unattended
  • All fires must be fully extinguished before leaving
  • Fireworks (including sparklers) are prohibited
  • One camping unit per campsite
  • Keep all vehicles and activities within your assigned site
  • Store trash securely away from wildlife
  • Replace moved picnic tables
